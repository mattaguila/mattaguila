    This is the date on which the license rights begin. If you don't know the exact start date, please make the best estimate you can.

  • Duration: Question

    Duration

    Select the length of time you will employ this particular license. Duration specifies the time period in which the licensed reproductions of an image will be in circulation. 3. Target Market

Edit
  • Territory: Question

    Territory

    Choose one or more geographical regions in which your image will appear. You may choose from the drop-down list or begin typing a territory into the field .If you are licensing for the web, select a territory that best represents the location of your primary audience. For example, if you're licensing for a company website that does a majority of its business in the US, Canada and United Kingdom, select those territories.

  • Industry: Question

    Industry

    Select the primary industry, product or service you're promoting.

    Why are we asking for this?

    For some of our RM images, exclusive rights are available for purchase. This means that the availability of an image may be limited to a specific market or territory. An individual or company granted an exclusive RM license is the only one allowed to use a specific image for a duration of time. Knowing what industry, product or service the image you want to license is intended for will help us determine if the image is currently restricted for use by an exclusivity license.
