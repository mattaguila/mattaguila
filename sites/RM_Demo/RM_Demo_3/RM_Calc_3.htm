4. Availability

Edit
  • Territory: Question

    Territory

    Choose one or more geographical regions in which your image will appear. You may choose from the drop-down list or begin typing a territory into the field .If you are licensing for the web, select a territory that best represents the location of your primary audience. For example, if you're licensing for a company website that does a majority of its business in the US, Canada and England, select those territories.

  • Start Date: Question

    Start Date

    The start date is the first date the image will be distributed. If you don't know the exact start date, please make the best estimate you can.

  • Duration: Question

    Duration

    Select the length of time you will employ this particular license. Duration specifies how long the licensed reproductions of an image will be in circulation. For example, a direct mail piece that will be sent to a different group of people every month for a year has a duration of one year.

  • Industry: Question

    Industry

    Select the primary industry, product or service you're promoting.

    Why are we asking for this?

    For some of our RM images, exclusive rights are available for purchase. This means that the availability of an image may be limited to a specific market or territory. An individual or company granted an exclusive RM license is the only one allowed to use a specific image for a duration of time. Knowing what industry, product or service the image you want to license is intended for will help us determine if the image is currently restricted for use by an exclusivity license.
