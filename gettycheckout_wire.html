GETTY IMAGES

Desktop Wireframes - Checkout Experience

I created a single checkout experience with corresponding branding for each of Getty Images's products including Getty Images, iStock and Thinkstock websites. Services included user research, form field auditing, sketches, desktop and mobile wireframes, prototypes, usability tests and high fidelity comps with redlines. Quantitative data was used to reach educated design decisions that proved to be effective at reaching our customers' needs.
